About us


History

The Wine House has been serving customers from around the world for over 40 years from our 18,000 square foot facility in West Los Angeles, CA. We provide a unique, exciting shopping environment for clients ranging in wine experience from the complete novice to the connoisseur. Our vast selection of over 6,000 domestic and imported wines, spirits and beers is one of the largest and well-curated in the country.




Service

When you need help making your wine selection, our nationally recognized staff of experts is ready to help. Our team of highly experienced and knowledgeable wine professionals will assist you in choosing the right product for any occasion. Whether you are a sophisticated vinophile or a casual connoisseur, our goal is to make your shopping experience enjoyable and efficient.
